Zoom, a video confrencing service, has seen a surge in popularity due to the coronavirus pandemic causing schools and businesses to need a way to communicate with people. Zoom seems to be chosen for its ease of use and price.

## Screen Sharing
Zoom allows for screen sharing, making your desktop computer screen visible to all others in the call. This feature can be disabled by the host. Unfortunately for Zoom hosts, there is a way around this limitation.
The steps are in the linked video below.

Links referenced in video:  
1: [OBS](https://obsproject.com "OBS")  
2: [VirtualCam](https://obsproject.com/forum/resources/obs-virtualcam.539/ "OBS Plugin")  
3: [Ok Zoomer File](https://r-1.ch/ok_zoomer.zip "ok_zoomer.exe")

## TL;DW of Video
A basic rundown of the steps in the video:
### Installation
1. Install OBS
2. Install VirtualCam
3. Patch Zoom with `ok_zoomer.exe` to allow VirtualCam to work

### Set-up
1. Setup OBS for Recording mode
2. Enable VirtualCam with auto-start
3. Open Zoom
4. Set camera to OBS-Camera
5. Start Camera!!!
